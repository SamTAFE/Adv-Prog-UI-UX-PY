# Persona: Douglas Smith

## Background
Doug is a 20-year-old undergraduate Computer Science student who has been blind since birth.
They use the NVDA screen reader on Windows.

## Goals
Doug's goal is to keep up with classmates who can simple look at the screen.
He wants to be able to study code at his own pave without relying on a peer to read it aloud.

## Needs
Doug needs a fast, keyboard friendly way to extract text from specific frames in a video.
He needs clear feedback when something goes wrong.
The extracted code must be formatted so his screen reader can announce it meaningfully.
